The N Token

NERA Camp & DAO Contribution Token

What is the N Token?

The N Token is the first layer of the Contribution Economy, the Genesis Token of the New Earth Rainbow Alliance. It is:

  • Non-financial
  • Non-transferable
  • Created for participants, stewards, and team members
  • A representation of energy, presence, and devotion

This token reflects your contribution to the foundations of the New Earth Rainbow Alliance.

What This Token Represents

  • Participation
  • Contribution
  • Willingness to learn
  • Support in producing the camp
  • Service roles
  • Engagement in NERA DAO incubation
  • Alignment with the New Earth vision

Note: It is not a utility, governance, or financial token. It is a pure reflection of contribution.

Why This Token Matters

  • Forms the initial circle of contributors
  • Founding participants learn systems, co-create the retreat, support the camp, activate sanctuary nodes, and contribute to DAO blueprint
  • Early devotion forms the foundation for future token layers

This Token Is the Gateway to the Next Phase

Holding the N Token places you in the Founding Whitelist for the next stage: the launch of the NERA Token. You will be invited to:

  • Give feedback on tokenomics
  • Participate in co-design
  • Shape parameters for Token #2
  • Decide fairness and improvements
  • Vote on foundational directions

You become a co-architect of the NERA DAO and token ecosystem.

How You Earn This Token at Camp

  • Support camp production
  • Contribute to temple activations
  • Assist with logistics
  • Help the community
  • Complete learning quests
  • Engage in DAO incubation
  • Take on service roles
  • Show leadership and initiative
  • Participate fully in the experience

Every act of participation matters and is documented.
