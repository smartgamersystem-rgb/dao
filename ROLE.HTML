Solar Entry

Members arrive as the Sun: radiant, ready to contribute skills, ideas, proposals, and presence. Roles are clear and integrated into governance and community space.

Lunar Exit

Members entering a Lunar phase pause active roles, transfer responsibilities, or store contribution credits safely. They can return later fully ready to contribute.

The system protects DAO stability and investor confidence: operations continue smoothly and contributions are preserved.
